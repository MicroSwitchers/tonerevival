Speaker Refoaming Tool

1. Test Tones

Use these tones to check for scraping or misalignment during the refoaming process.

30 Hz Tone: Good for detecting low-frequency issues.

Pink Noise: Useful for overall response and detecting issues across the frequency spectrum.

2. Frequency Sweep

Use the sweep to test the full range of the speaker after refoaming.

Duration: 10s

Adjust the frequency range and duration as needed. Looping the sweep can help identify persistent issues.

3. Volume Control

- +

Adjust the volume carefully. The 30 Hz tone can reach much higher volumes than other sounds.

Warning: High volume may cause distortion or damage. Reduce if necessary.

How to Use

  1. Start with the 30 Hz tone at a low volume to check for any scraping or misalignment.
  2. Use pink noise to assess overall speaker response and identify any issues across the frequency range.
  3. After refoaming, use the frequency sweep to test the full range of the speaker.
  4. Adjust the sweep settings as needed for more detailed testing of specific frequency ranges.
  5. Always start with lower volumes and increase gradually to avoid damage to the speaker or your hearing.
